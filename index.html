Why Copenhagen?

Copenhagen, the city of Spirels

Known as the City of Spires, Copenhagen’s skyline is adorned with towers that echo its rich history. From the twisting spire of Our Saviour’s Church to the regal green towers of Rosenborg Castle, these architectural gems are a testament to the city’s evolution from a Viking fishing village in the 10th century to Denmark’s vibrant capital. Walking through Nyhavn, with its colorful 17th-century houses lining the canals, or climbing the Round Tower for sweeping views of terracotta rooftops and the Øresund Strait, you’ll see how history and modernity coexist in harmony. Beyond its beauty, Copenhagen exudes the Danish spirit of hygge—a sense of warmth and coziness found in candlelit cafes, tranquil parks, and the charm of Tivoli Gardens. It’s a city that invites you to slow down, explore its timeless streets, and embrace its serene, yet dynamic atmosphere .

Keffebar Riccos

The simple truth is that every week, Ricco still stands and roasts the beans you drink at the coffee bars, and he hasn’t become any less focused or determined over the years.
And you can taste it. And yes, we say that with pride.
When it says Ricco’s on the facade, it’s truly a signature of work delivered.

Stefansgade 15, 2200 København

Seks Bakery & Eatery

Seks Bakery & Eatery is a delightful blend of warmth, flavor, and creativity. Known for its artisan bread, mouthwatering pastries, and cozy yet modern ambiance, this spot is perfect for both a quick coffee stop or a leisurely meal. The open kitchen invites you to witness the magic behind their freshly baked goods, while the friendly atmosphere makes it a place you’ll want to return to.

Adress

Krystalgade 6, København K

What I like about it

I love them for its dedication to quality and detail, evident in every perfectly baked loaf and flaky pastry.
